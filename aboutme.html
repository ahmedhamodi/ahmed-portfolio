Work

Over my high school journey, and soon to come university journey, I've worked at a few places, which has given me some well rounded experience. My first and longest standing work experience would be with the OSA (Ontario Soccer Association) as a soccer referee. I've been refereeing with the OSA for nearly four years now, officiating over 500 games of various age groups. This has been a very rewarding experience as it has built my confidence, responsibility, leadership and teamwork skills.

My other job was a summer student job with Aeryon Labs Inc. During this job, I learned what it is like working a job in a high tech industry, working closely with some of the engineers, quality staff and managers in the company. Aeryon is a relatively small company still, so being in that closely knit community was very beneficial as I was able to communicate and socialize with all sectors of a business. Learning about the people and the work they do is truly a once in a lifetime experience for a high school student.
